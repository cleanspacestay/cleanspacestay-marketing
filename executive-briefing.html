What actually moves the number

Two companies can earn the same profit and sell for very different prices.

Specialty contractors typically sell for 3.5–7× earnings (EBITDA). Where a company lands is mostly about how de-risked and transferable it looks — and nearly every driver is a systems-and-data problem.
