The Core Problem

You bid complex jobs with assumptions. Then you fly blind.

Every project starts with estimated labor, materials, travel, subcontractors, and schedule assumptions. Then the company struggles to know in real time whether actual performance is still on track.

What was estimated

Labor, materials, equipment, travel, subs, contingency

What's actually happening

Actual cost, committed cost, remaining cost, schedule progress

The CEO dashboard answers: are we still on track to finish profitably? — for every project, in real time.
