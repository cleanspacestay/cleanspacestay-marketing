The Intelligence Layer

AI that watches every project 24/7.

Like having a CFO-level analyst monitoring every cost line, every schedule slip, every change order — and surfacing what matters before you ask.

Budget Alert

MIA Tower labor trending 9.5% over estimate

3 electricians added last week to recover schedule. At current burn rate, labor will exceed budget by $380K at completion. Recommend reviewing scope with PM before next draw.

Schedule Risk

ORD Warehouse has 4 open RFIs blocking procurement

If RFI-012 and RFI-014 are not resolved by Friday, structural steel PO cannot issue — likely 2-week delay. Similar pattern on ATL project last year led to 3-week slip.

Change Order Draft

CO-002 HVAC upgrade: AI-drafted description ready

Based on RFI-008 response and drawing rev C, drafted change order description with cost breakdown: $52K materials, $28K labor, $5K engineering. Awaiting PM review.

Weekly Report

Executive weekly report generated

Portfolio summary: 3 of 5 projects on track, 2 flagged. Total forecast profit $2.8M (down $180K from last week). Cash position stable. AR aging improved on 2 accounts.

AI recommends. Humans approve. The AI Router can summarize, draft, compare, forecast, and flag risk — but it cannot approve purchase orders, release invoices, alter contract values, or mark projects complete. Every financial action requires human approval.
