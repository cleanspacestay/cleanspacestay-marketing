The Problem

Managing Workforce Travel Shouldn't Be This Hard

If you're still using spreadsheets, email threads, and sticky notes to coordinate lodging for field workers — you're losing time and money.

Scattered Information

Booking confirmations in email, worker lists in Excel, budgets in another spreadsheet. Nothing talks to each other.

Hours Wasted Weekly

Coordinators spend significant time each week manually tracking who's staying where, what's confirmed, and what's over budget.

Budget Overruns

Without real-time visibility into spend per project, you don't know you're over budget until the invoice arrives.

No Accountability

When something goes wrong — a missed booking, a wrong hotel — there's no audit trail to figure out what happened.

Features

Everything You Need to Manage Workforce Travel

Purpose-built for companies that send workers to job sites across the country.

Hotel Booking Management

Track every reservation with confirmation numbers, rates, check-in/out dates, and guest assignments. Never lose a booking again.

  • Confirmation number tracking
  • Rate and tax breakdown
  • Cancellation deadline alerts
  • Multi-room group bookings

Flight Bookings

Manage airline reservations, record locators, and itineraries for traveling workers.

Booking Requests

Workers and PMs submit requests that flow through approval workflows before becoming bookings.

Budget Tracking

Set budgets per project and category. Get alerts when spend approaches limits — before it's too late.

Worker Management

Maintain a directory of all field workers with contact info, assignments, and travel history.

Reports & Analytics

See spend by project, by worker, by vendor. Identify savings opportunities and budget trends.

Audit Trail

Every action is logged. Know who booked what, when it was approved, and who made changes.
