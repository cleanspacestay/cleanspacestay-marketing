Mobile App

Field Worker Guide

Everything you need is in the mobile app. No passwords — just your phone number.

1Getting Started

  1. Download the app — Search "CleanSpace Stay" on the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play (Android)
  2. Sign in — Enter your phone number. You'll receive a one-time code via text or WhatsApp. Enter it.
  3. You're in — No password to remember. You stay logged in until you sign out.

2Requesting Travel

You can request your own travel, or your Foreman can do it for you.

  1. Tap Requests at the bottom of the app
  2. Tap New Request
  3. Select your Project (the job you're traveling for)
  4. Pick your travel dates (start and end)
  5. Choose what you need: Hotel, Flight, Rental Car, or Rideshare
  6. Add any notes (hotel preferences, early check-in, etc.)
  7. Review everything, then tap Submit
Submit Request → PM Approves → Ops Books It → You Get Notified

3Viewing Your Trips

Tap Trips to see all your upcoming and past travel. Each trip shows:

  • Hotel name, address, and confirmation number
  • Flight details and times
  • Rental car pickup/return info
  • Check-in and check-out dates

4Checking In & Out

When you arrive at your hotel (or pick up your rental car, or board your flight):

  1. Open your trip
  2. Tap Check In on the booking
  3. When you leave, tap Check Out

This lets ops know you arrived safely and helps track actual travel dates.

5Messages

Need to ask a question or report an issue? Tap Messages to chat directly with ops. You'll also receive notifications here when your bookings are confirmed or changed.

6Your Profile

Tap Profile to update:

  • Your photo (used for identification)
  • Driver's license (front and back photos)
  • TSA Known Traveler / Global Entry number
  • Loyalty program numbers (Marriott Bonvoy, Hilton Honors, Delta SkyMiles, etc.)

Keeping this updated means ops can book using your loyalty numbers and preferences automatically.

Mobile App + Web

Foreman Guide

You manage travel for your crew. Submit requests for them, approve when needed, and keep everyone on track.

1Getting Started

  1. Download the app — Same as workers: App Store or Google Play, search "CleanSpace Stay"
  2. Sign in with your phone number — OTP code, no password
  3. You'll see the same tabs as workers, plus an Approvals tab

You can also access the web dashboard at app.cleanspacestay.com for a bigger view.

2Requesting Travel for Your Crew

As a Foreman, you can submit travel requests for yourself AND your crew members:

  1. Tap Requests → New Request
  2. Select the Project
  3. In the Traveler field, search for the worker(s) who need travel
  4. Pick dates, travel types, and add notes
  5. Review and Submit

You can select multiple travelers in one request if they're all traveling to the same place on the same dates.

3Approving Requests

When one of your workers submits their own request, it lands in your Approvals tab:

  1. Tap Approvals
  2. Review the request (who, where, when, what type)
  3. Tap Approve, Reject, or Request Changes

Once approved, ops will book it and the worker gets notified.

4Viewing Your Crew's Bookings

On the web dashboard, you can see all bookings for workers on your project(s). This includes hotel confirmations, flight details, and check-in status.

5Everything Else

You also have access to:

  • Messages — Chat with ops and your workers
  • Calendar — See upcoming travel across your crew
  • Map — See where your workers are located
  • Check-in — Same as workers, for your own bookings
  • Profile — Update your own info and loyalty numbers
Mobile App + Web

Project Manager Guide

You oversee travel for your project(s). Approve requests, track budgets, and keep visibility on all crew movement.

1Getting Started

  1. Web: Log in at app.cleanspacestay.com with your email and password
  2. App: Download CleanSpace Stay, sign in with your phone number (OTP)

The web dashboard gives you the full view. The app is great for approving requests on the go.

2Approval Queue

Your primary action item. When foremen or workers submit travel requests for your project, they appear here:

  1. Go to Approval Queue in the sidebar
  2. Review each request: traveler, project, dates, travel type, notes
  3. Approve — ops will book it
  4. Reject — traveler is notified with your reason
  5. Request Changes — ask for different dates, hotel, etc.

3Budget Tracking

See how much has been spent on travel for your project vs. what was budgeted:

  • Budget Tracking in the sidebar shows spend by category (lodging, flights, transportation, per diem)
  • Compare actual vs. budgeted amounts
  • Drill into individual charges

4Your Project Dashboard

Go to Projects to see and manage your project(s):

  • View all workers assigned to the project
  • See active bookings and upcoming travel
  • Edit project details (location, dates, contacts)
  • Add or remove workers from the project

Admin / Operations Guide

You run the day-to-day. Book travel, manage workers, handle approvals, and keep everything flowing.

1Getting Started

  1. Log in at app.cleanspacestay.com with your email and password
  2. You have full access to every screen in the sidebar

2Daily Workflow

Your typical day:

  1. Check Approval Queue — approve or route pending requests
  2. Book approved travel — create hotel/flight/car bookings from approved requests
  3. Handle messages — respond to worker questions
  4. Monitor notifications — check-in confirmations, status changes
  5. Email Import — forward booking confirmations to auto-parse into the system

3Managing Bookings

Each booking type has its own screen (Hotel, Flight, Car, Rideshare). From any booking screen:

  • Create — Add a new booking manually
  • Edit — Update dates, confirmation numbers, notes
  • Cancel — Mark as cancelled
  • View details — Click any row to open the detail drawer (who, what, when, status)

4Managing the Team

Go to Team to:

  • Invite new users — set their role (PM, Foreman, Worker, Accounting)
  • Edit roles — promote/demote as needed
  • Disable accounts — when someone leaves the company
  • Permission overrides — grant specific screen access to individual users beyond their role default

5Projects & Workers

  • Projects — Create projects, assign PMs and Foremen, set budgets, manage worker assignments
  • Workers — View all workers, their profiles, loyalty numbers, assigned projects
  • Contacts — Hotel contacts, vendor reps, emergency contacts
  • Vendors — Manage preferred hotels, airlines, rental companies

Accounting Guide

You reconcile travel charges with QuickBooks, track budgets, and run financial reports.

1Getting Started

  1. Log in at app.cleanspacestay.com with your email and password
  2. Your primary screens are: Reconciliation, Budget Tracking, and Reports

2Reconciliation

This is where QuickBooks transactions meet platform bookings:

  1. Go to Reconciliation in the sidebar
  2. You'll see QB transactions that need to be matched to bookings
  3. For each transaction: Match it to the correct booking, or mark as Disputed / No Charge Expected
  4. Once matched, mark as Reconciled
Pending → Matched → Reconciled

3Budget Tracking

View and manage travel budgets by project:

  • See budget vs. actual spend per project
  • Break down by category: lodging, flights, transportation, per diem
  • Flag projects that are over budget

4Reports

Full access to all reports:

  • Travel spend by project, by worker, by vendor
  • Reconciliation status reports
  • Budget variance reports
  • Export to CSV for further analysis

Frequently Asked Questions

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
On the web login page, click "Forgot Password" and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link. Mobile users don't have passwords — just sign in with your phone number again.
I'm not receiving the OTP code on my phone.
Make sure the phone number on your account is correct (ask your admin to verify). Check if you have SMS or WhatsApp blocked. If using WhatsApp, make sure WhatsApp is installed. Contact ops if the issue persists.
How do I change my phone number?
Ask your Admin to update your phone number in the Team settings. You'll need to verify the new number with an OTP on next login.
Can I request travel for someone else?
Foremen can request for their crew. Project Managers and Admins can request for anyone. Field Workers can only request for themselves.
How do I know when my booking is confirmed?
You'll receive a push notification on the app and an in-app notification. You can also check your Trips tab — confirmed bookings show the confirmation number and all details.
What if I need to change my travel dates?
Message ops through the Messages tab in the app. They'll update the booking and you'll be notified of the change.
The app says I have no projects. What do I do?
Your Admin needs to assign you to a project. Contact your Foreman or ops and they'll add you.
How do I add my hotel loyalty number?
Go to Profile in the app, scroll down to the loyalty programs section, and enter your numbers (Marriott Bonvoy, Hilton Honors, etc.). Ops will use these when booking your hotels.
Is the app available in Spanish?
Yes. The app supports English and Spanish. Your language preference is set in your profile.
